The ACM AI Lab is co-organizing a BMVC2024 workshop (https://rrow2024.github.io/) end of November in Glasgow Scotland.
<br><br>
We have a high caliber line up plenary speakers:
<br><br>
• Jiří Matas (Faculty of Information Technology CTU in Prague)
<br><br>
• Petra Bevandic (Bielefeld University)
<br><br>
• Robert Geirhos (Google DeepMind)
<br><br>
• Toby Breckon (Durham University)
<br><br>
We encourage researchers to submit their original works on robust recognition in the open world to our workshop. Your manuscripts will undergo a high quality peer review. Accepted works will be published in the BMVC2024 workshop proceedings. The submission deadline is the 18th of August, see https://rrow2024.github.io/ for details. We are looking forward to your submissions and hope to see you at BMVC2024!
