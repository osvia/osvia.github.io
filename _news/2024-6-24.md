The ACM AI Lab, part of the Interdisciplinary Center for Machine Learning and Data Analytics (IZMD), took part in the University of Wuppertal's summer fest. This event provided an excellent opportunity for our team to showcase innovative projects and actively engage with the university community. Additionally, we shared our booth with the TMDT, further enriching the experience with a diverse array of technological demonstrations.
<br><br>
Our primary objectives were to introduce our team to a wider audience, foster connections, and spark interest in the field of artificial intelligence. To achieve these goals, we presented several interactive demonstrations.
<br><br>
The main attraction at our booth was a driving simulator equipped with AI vision technology. Visitors experienced firsthand how AI vision systems, particularly semantic segmentation, operate. This hands-on demo was both entertaining and educational, illustrating practical AI applications.
<br><br>
Additionally, we featured a Generative Adversarial Network (GAN) puzzle game, which further engaged visitors and demonstrated the creative potential of AI.
