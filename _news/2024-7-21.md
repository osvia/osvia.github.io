Our work with colleagues from EPFL's CVLab has been accepted for publication at BMVC, about which I am very glad! It is entitled "AttEntropy: Segmenting Unknown Objects in Complex Scenes using the Spatial Attention Entropy of Semantic Segmentation Transformers," authored by Krzysztof Lis, myself, Annika MÃ¼tze, Sina Honari, Pascal Fua & Mathieu Salzmann. We studied properties of attention-based segmentation transformers and had this cool finding that attention trained on semantic segmentation of street scenes can segment any object in any context.
